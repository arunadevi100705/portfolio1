01

Alcohol Detection System

Integrated breathalyzer sensor near the rider's mouth. If blood alcohol levels exceed legal parameters, the ignition transmission signals are blocked, preventing engine start.

02

Helmet Compliance Monitor

Uses smart switches/pressure sensors inside the padding and buckle mechanisms to guarantee that the helmet is correctly worn and strapped before the vehicle registers as operational.

03

IoT Alert Transmitter

Embedded accelerometers monitor collision-grade impact force. In case of anomaly, a GPS-equipped cellular module transmits emergency alerts with location details to nearby emergency services.
